How every line gets scored

The pre-pick signals BoardWise uses to rank the board.

Model probability

What the model estimates.

A win probability before comparing the play with the sportsbook price.

Market edge

Price versus probability.

The gap between model probability and the probability implied by the posted line.

Expected value

The edge in return terms.

Expected return at the available price, shown before a pick is made.

Wise Choice™ tiers

Signal buckets, not certainty.

Every opportunity lands in a tier from No Edge to Prime. A stronger tier is still a probability, not a promise, and never personalized stake advice.

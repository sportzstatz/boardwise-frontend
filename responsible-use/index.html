Responsible Use

Last updated: June 29, 2026

BoardWise is a sports-forecast analytics service. BoardWise does not accept wagers, place bets, custody customer funds, operate a sportsbook, run contests, charge entry fees for prizes, or award monetary or material prizes.

BoardWise displays model forecasts, probabilities, market comparisons, expected value, signal strength, tracking records, and related analytics for informational and entertainment purposes. These materials are not guarantees and are not personalized advice.

Use BoardWise responsibly

  • Do not use BoardWise if you are under 18.
  • If you choose to place wagers elsewhere, do so only where legal, only if you meet the legal wagering age, and only through licensed operators where required.
  • Do not treat BoardWise forecasts, units, signal strength, or expected value as a promise of profit or as personalized stake advice.
  • Do not wager money you cannot afford to lose.
  • Do not chase losses.
  • Do not use gambling as a way to solve financial, emotional, or personal problems.
  • Consider taking breaks from sports-wagering content if it affects your finances, relationships, work, school, sleep, or mental health.

No personalized wagering or bankroll advice

BoardWise does not know your financial situation, risk tolerance, location, sportsbook access, or legal eligibility. BoardWise does not provide dollar-denominated stake recommendations and does not let users enter bankrolls.

Any displayed unit, edge, expected value, or signal-strength information is a standardized analytics label, not an instruction to wager any amount.

If gambling may be causing harm

Help is available.

National Problem Gambling Helpline: call or text 1-800-MY-RESET
Iowa gambling help: call 1-800-BETS-OFF, text 855-895-8398, or visit Your Life Iowa gambling resources

These resources are third-party services. BoardWise is not affiliated with them and does not control their availability or advice.

Account support

BoardWise does not currently offer a formal voluntary account-lock program. If BoardWise receives a support request indicating that use of the Service may be contributing to gambling harm, BoardWise may provide responsible-use resources, suggest cancellation, or take other reasonable account action at its discretion.

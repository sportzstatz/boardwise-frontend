01

What BoardWise is — and isn't

BoardWise is a forecasting and analysis product. It estimates probabilities, compares them with market prices, and ranks opportunities. It is for informational and entertainment purposes only. It is not a sportsbook, does not accept bets, and never guarantees an outcome.

02

Signals are not certainties

Our tiers — from No Edge to Prime — are signal buckets, not guarantees. A stronger signal is still a probability, not a promise, and a higher score does not automatically mean higher historical ROI. Never wager money you cannot afford to lose, and never chase losses.

03

Set your own limits

Before you play, decide how much time and money you're comfortable spending, and treat it as the cost of entertainment. Most sportsbooks offer deposit limits, time-outs, and self-exclusion tools — use them. Your BoardWise access is separate from any wagering account.

04

Know the warning signs

Consider stepping back if you:

  • Bet more than you planned, or bet to win back losses.
  • Borrow money or fall behind on bills to wager.
  • Feel anxious, restless, or secretive about betting.
  • Let wagering interfere with work, sleep, or relationships.

Protecting minors

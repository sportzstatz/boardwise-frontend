What BoardWise is

BoardWise is a sports forecasting product. A model estimates a win probability for each game, compares it with the sportsbook price, and ranks the resulting opportunities by edge, expected value, and Kelly strength. The output is analysis — one clear read per board — not a wager, a recommendation to bet, or a promise of profit.

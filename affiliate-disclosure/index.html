Affiliate & Sponsorship Disclosure

Where BoardWise stands on sportsbook compensation, referrals, and third-party marks.

Last updated June 29, 2026 ~2 min read
On this page
01Compensation status 02If this changes 03Third-party marks

BoardWise may display sportsbook names or market prices to identify where odds data is reported by third-party data sources. Unless expressly stated otherwise, BoardWise is not affiliated with, sponsored by, endorsed by, or approved by any sportsbook, league, team, player association, or data provider.

01

Current sportsbook compensation status

BoardWise does not currently receive sportsbook affiliate commissions, referral fees, cost-per-click payments, cost-per-acquisition payments, deposit-based compensation, wager-based compensation, loss-based compensation, revenue share, free sportsbook consideration, paid placement fees, or sportsbook sponsorship compensation.

BoardWise does not currently use sportsbook referral codes, sportsbook promo codes, paid outbound sportsbook links, tracked sportsbook links, or sportsbook-compensated rankings.

BoardWise does not allow compensation to influence which sportsbook appears as “best available,” how odds are ranked, or which forecasts are displayed.

02

If this changes

If BoardWise enters into an affiliate, sponsorship, advertising, paid-placement, referral, or similar relationship, BoardWise will update this disclosure. BoardWise will also place clear and conspicuous disclosures near affected links, rankings, recommendations, placements, emails, social posts, or ads where required or appropriate.

03

Third-party marks

Sportsbook names, league names, team names, logos, and other marks belong to their respective owners. Use of a name or mark does not imply sponsorship, endorsement, approval, or affiliation.

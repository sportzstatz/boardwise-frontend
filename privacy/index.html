Privacy Policy

How BoardWise collects, uses, and protects the limited information tied to your account.

Last updated June 30, 2026 ~5 min read

BoardWise is a data-first product with a deliberately small footprint of personal information. This policy explains what we collect when you sign in and use the board, why we collect it, and the choices you have.

Important 21+ · Informational only

BoardWise is for informational and entertainment purposes only. It is not gambling, financial, legal, or investment advice, and no pick is guaranteed. You must be 21+ and legally permitted to use wagering-related content.

01

Information we collect

We keep the account footprint small and collect only what the board needs to run.

  • Account — your email address, used for magic-link sign-in. No passwords are stored.
  • Plan & access — your tier (guest, free, Founder, admin) and the boards it unlocks.
  • Usage — pages viewed, boards opened, and markets expanded, used to improve the product.
  • Device & log — IP address, browser type, and timestamps, kept for security and diagnostics.

We do not store your payment card. Billing, when applicable, is handled by our payment processor.

02

How we use information

  • Authenticate your sign-in and keep your session secure.
  • Provide plan-appropriate board access and remember your view preferences.
  • Measure how the board is used so we can rank and present opportunities more clearly.
  • Detect abuse, prevent fraud, and meet legal obligations.

We never use your information to make guarantees about outcomes. Signal buckets are not promises.

03

Cookies & analytics

BoardWise uses a small number of first-party cookies to keep you signed in and to remember view settings. Aggregate, privacy-respecting analytics help us understand which boards and markets are used. You can clear cookies in your browser at any time; doing so will sign you out.

04

How we share information

We do not sell your personal data. We share limited information only with:

  • Service providers who host, email, and process payments on our behalf, under contract.
  • Authorities, when required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users and BoardWise.
  • A successor entity in the event of a merger or acquisition, subject to this policy.
05

Data retention

We keep account information for as long as your account is active, and usage and log data for a limited period needed for security and product improvement. You can ask us to delete your account at any time.

06

Your choices & rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information. To exercise any of these, email support@useboardwise.com from your account address. We honor verified requests within a reasonable time.

07

Security

Access is protected by magic-link authentication and encrypted transport, and we limit internal access on a need-to-know basis. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

08

Age & eligibility

BoardWise is intended only for adults 21 and older who are legally permitted to view wagering-related content. It is not directed to anyone under 21, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

09

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date, and where appropriate we will notify you in the product.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026 · Effective date: June 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BoardWise Analytics LLC (“BoardWise,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, shares, and retains information when you use useboardwise.com and related BoardWise websites, dashboards, accounts, subscriptions, and services (collectively, the “Service”).

BoardWise is operated by BoardWise Analytics LLC, an Iowa limited liability company. Contact us at support@useboardwise.com.

1. Summary

BoardWise collects limited information needed to provide accounts, authentication, subscription access, security, support, and sports-forecast analytics. BoardWise does not currently sell personal information, share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising, use advertising pixels, use session replay, or offer SMS alerts.

BoardWise does not accept wagers, place bets, custody customer funds, operate a sportsbook, run contests, or award prizes.

2. Information we collect

Account information

We may collect your email address, canonicalized email address, optional display name, account status, access level, plan, entitlements, marketing preference, consent records, and related account metadata.

Authentication and security information

We may collect information related to passwordless login, magic-link challenges, login timestamps, hashed session tokens, hashed login tokens, authentication state, failed login attempts, abuse-prevention records, account-suppression records, and other security records.

Payment and subscription information

When paid subscriptions are enabled, payments will be processed by Stripe or another payment processor. We may receive and store Stripe customer IDs, subscription IDs, price IDs, checkout session IDs, invoice or payment status, billing periods, subscription status, payment failure status, refund or dispute status, limited payment-method details such as card brand or last four digits if made available by Stripe, and related webhook or reconciliation records.

BoardWise does not intend to store full credit card numbers or full payment credentials on its own servers.

Usage, device, and log information

We and our service providers may collect server logs and technical information such as IP address, hashed IP address where configured, browser type, user-agent string, device information, timestamps, pages viewed, referring page, request URLs, API requests, error records, security events, cookie identifiers, and similar diagnostic or operational information.

Support and correspondence

If you contact us, we may collect your message, email address, issue description, attachments you provide, and our responses.

Cookies and similar technologies

We may use cookies or similar technologies that are necessary for authentication, session management, security, fraud prevention, user preferences, and Service functionality. Cloudflare and other infrastructure providers may also use cookies or similar technologies for security and performance.

BoardWise does not currently use advertising pixels, session replay, Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, TikTok Pixel, or cross-site behavioral advertising trackers. If that changes, this Privacy Policy should be updated before those tools are enabled.

Information we do not intend to collect

BoardWise does not intend to collect Social Security numbers, government IDs, bank account numbers, exact geolocation for wagering verification, health information, biometric information, or sensitive personal information unless this Privacy Policy is updated and a lawful basis exists.

BoardWise does not currently allow users to save picks, favorite teams, bankrolls, dollar-denominated stake preferences, alerts, profiles, comments, leaderboards, shared slips, or other user-generated content.

With your direction or consent

6. No sale or targeted advertising sharing

BoardWise does not currently sell personal information and does not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising or targeted advertising. BoardWise does not currently permit sportsbook compensation, affiliate tracking, advertising pixels, or cross-site behavioral advertising on the Service.

7. Retention

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including to provide the Service, maintain accounts, process subscriptions, comply with law, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, prevent abuse, and maintain security.

Typical retention principles include:

  • Account information: retained while your account exists and for a reasonable period after deletion if needed for legal, security, tax, payment, dispute, or backup purposes.
  • Authentication challenges and sessions: retained for a limited period appropriate for security and troubleshooting.
  • Logs and security records: retained for a reasonable period for security, debugging, abuse prevention, and operational integrity.
  • Payment, subscription, invoice, tax, consent, and webhook records: retained as needed for payment processing, tax, accounting, legal compliance, fraud prevention, chargebacks, and dispute handling.
  • Support messages: retained for a reasonable period to manage support, disputes, safety, and business records.
  • Backups: retained until overwritten or deleted through regular backup rotation.

When you request deletion, BoardWise will delete or deidentify account information where reasonably feasible, but may retain limited records where needed for legal, tax, accounting, security, fraud, dispute, enforcement, backup, or legitimate business purposes.

BoardWise is a small business and may not be subject to every state privacy law threshold, but we will review and respond to privacy requests in good faith.
